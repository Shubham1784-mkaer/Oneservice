Our Process

  1. Inspection & quote

    We inspect your item & share a repair quote for approval.

  2. Quote approval

    You can approve the quote to proceed, or pay a visitation charge of rupees 99 if declined.

  3. Repair & spare parts

    If needed, we will source spare parts from local markets.

  4. Replacement, if needed

    If repair is not possible, we will replace the item.

  5. Warranty activation

    The service is covered by a 30-day warranty for any issues after repair.

General Information

  1. The service is covered by a 30-day warranty for any issues after repair/replacement.

    We will fix it for free.

  2. The cost do not include spare parts.

    The amount you pay at booking is a visitation fee which will be adjusted in your final replacement quote.

  3. One Service does not offer warranty on spare parts purchased from local market or provided by the customer.
